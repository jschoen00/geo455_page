As a GIS intern with the Upper Midwest Environmental Science Center (UMESC), I get to work on a variety of GIS-related projects. I have assisted in orthorectification of aerial imagery of the Mississippi River for US Army Corps of Engineers, helped create a database with geospatial data of mussel species found in the Mississippi, and most recently, I am digitizing historic fire documents to make them easily accessible for the public.



As an intern with Global Treks and Adventures, I went on a research expedition in January of 2022 to the U.S. Virgin Islands. While on the expedition, I snorkled in order to collect data on marine life, mapped hiking trails, and I am now using the hiking trail data to create professional maps of each trail to be used in a travel guide for the island of St. John.
